Melitta Deljanin is a New York based artist and designer.

Pareidolia is a music festival that brings people together through different interpretations of patterns and music. Pareidolia refers to the tendency of the human mind to perceive familiar patterns or shapes in random stimuli. It highlights the human brain’s remarkable ability to find meaning and structure in the ambiguous.

The animation is a superimposition of many different semi-transparent images, each one in a vibrant color. This results in an array of connectivity through the images, each one relating to the next, with the colors bleeding into each other. Through a colored filter it is possible to discover the layers in which the image is composed.

The filter’s colors are Red, Green and Blue, each one of them will reveal a different layer.

This app will transform your mobile device in a viewing lens to experience the world of Pareidolia.

Privacy Policy

When viewed on a device that supports camera access from a browser, the site will ask for permission to use the camera. The video stream is only viewable by you when you enable the camera access.

We do not save any video to your phone, and no video (or any other information about the video or you) is sent to us. Your privacy is important, so we keep it simple and do not collect any personal information.
